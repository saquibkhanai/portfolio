About Me

I'm a creative and detail-oriented Logo Designer with 2+ years of freelance experience delivering unique and tailored visual identities to clients on platforms like Fiverr, Facebook, and WhatsApp. I specialize in crafting custom logos that reflect brand values and stand out in competitive markets. With strong communication skills and a professional workflow, I ensure every design meets client expectations while maintaining high quality and timely delivery.

I also work extensively in 3D design, creating high-detail sculptures, 3D print-ready models, and textured assets for digital or physical use. Using tools like Blender, Substance Painter and ZBrush, I bring concepts to life with a focus on realism, form, and function. Whether it’s character models, props, or product prototypes, I aim to deliver clean, production-ready results for both digital visualization and physical fabrication.

In addition to logo design, I offer a range of 2D design services including minimalist logos, poster designs, food menus, and social media graphics. My approach combines simplicity with strong visual appeal, using effective color theory and typography to create designs that communicate clearly and leave a lasting impression.
