About Me

I'm Muhammad Saquib Khan, a 20-year-old 2D/3D artist and data science student currently completing my Bachelor's degree at IST Islamabad. Originally from the beautiful Azad Jammu Kashmir, I now reside in Rawalpindi where I balance my creative work with academic pursuits.

With over 2 years of professional experience on Fiverr, I've created stunning visual designs for clients worldwide. I specialize in 3D modeling, digital sculpture, logo design, and poster creation, with a portfolio that showcases my versatility and technical skills.

Currently in my 6th semester, I'm expanding my expertise into Python programming and data analysis. I believe that the intersection of art and data science holds incredible potential for innovation, and I'm excited to leverage both disciplines in my future work.
