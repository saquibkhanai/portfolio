About Me

I'm a professional 3D Modeler and Sculpture Artist with 3+ years of specialized experience in creating intricate 3D models exclusively for 3D printing. I specialize in transforming concepts into production-ready sculpts optimized for physical fabrication.

My expertise spans both organic character design and hard surface modeling, with a focus on creating models with perfect topology and structural integrity. I work exclusively with Blender and ZBrush to deliver models that meet the highest standards for 3D printing.

I approach every project with a problem-solving mindset, ensuring each sculpt is not only visually stunning but also technically sound for physical fabrication. Whether it's for collectibles, figurines, or prototypes, I deliver clean, optimized models ready for printing.
